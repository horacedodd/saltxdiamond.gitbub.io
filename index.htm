Bio_Data

Salt x Diamond was founded by Horace Dodd, a multi-hyphenate artist and entrepreneur redefining the intersection of entertainment and technology. As an actor, he has starred in acclaimed television and streaming series including Narcos, Mayor of Kingstown, Grown-ish, and The Family Business. His work as a songwriter and producer includes “Can’t Slow Down” for Fan Chengcheng, which peaked at #18 on the China Billboard Charts.

Now, through Salt x Diamond, a cutting-edge Gen AI creative firm, he pioneers new frontiers in storytelling and content creation, leveraging artificial intelligence to shape the future of digital entertainment. Salt x Diamond is a creative partner with prestige Gen AI companies Hailuo AI and LUMA Labs, collaborating at the forefront of AI-driven content innovation and immersive storytelling.
